# searchclaw

**searchclaw** 是一个专为科研人员打造的自动化文献检索与追踪工具。

本项目旨在通过自动化的手段，辅助科研工作者高效地获取每日感兴趣的最新论文，并针对特定领域（如 **ieal** 相关）进行深度文献挖掘。

---

## 🎯 项目目标

在信息爆炸的今天，科研人员往往难以从海量数据中快速筛选出有价值的内容。`searchclaw` 的核心目标是：

- **每日推送**：自动化抓取每日更新的感兴趣领域的论文，生成简报或推送。
- **定向挖掘**：针对特定项目或关键词（例如 `ieal` 相关研究）进行深度文献搜索与整理。
- **效率提升**：减少手动检索的时间，让科研人员专注于核心研究工作。

# searchclaw

**searchclaw** is an automated literature retrieval and tracking tool designed specifically for researchers.

This project aims to assist researchers in efficiently acquiring the latest papers of interest on a daily basis through automation, and to perform in-depth literature mining for specific domains (such as **ieal**-related research).

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

In today's era of information explosion, researchers often struggle to quickly filter out valuable content from massive amounts of data. The core goals of `searchclaw` are:

- **Daily Push Notifications**: Automatically crawl for updated papers in fields of interest and generate briefings or push notifications.
- **Targeted Mining**: Conduct in-depth literature searches and organization for specific projects or keywords (e.g., `ieal` related research).
- **Efficiency Improvement**: Reduce the time spent on manual retrieval, allowing researchers to focus on their core research work.
